Jab We Met (Original Motion Picture Soundtrack)

Pritam, Sandesh Sandilya
BOLLYWOOD · 2007

Pritam & Sandesh Sandilya are unstoppable in pop mode on the Jab We Met soundtrack—which was composed Sanjoy Chowdhury and one of the top-selling albums of 2007. They employ a light touch that lifts this romantic comedy score beyond the big-screen, giving it a life of its own. The bhangra club beats of “Mauja Hi Mauja”, Punjabi dance number “Nagada Nagada”, road-trip singalong favourite “Yeh Ishq Hai” (with an even livelier Dj A-Myth Bollywood remix included) and Rashid Khan’s moving Sufi influence on “Aaoge Jab Tum” all turn this album into unforgettable pop gold.

21 September 2007 10 songs, 47 minutes ℗ 2007 Super Cassettes Industries Private Limited
